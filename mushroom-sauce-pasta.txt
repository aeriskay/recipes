Ingredients:
 - 200g chopped onions
 - 260g sliced mushrooms
 - 1 teaspoon chopped garlic
 - 30g butter
 - 1 cup cream
 - Salt & pepper
 - Basil
 - Parmesan cheese (optional)

Directions:
  1. Cook pasta as per instructions on package
  2. Heat olive oil in non-stick skillet over medium heat
  3. Add onions and garlic. Cook for 4 minutes.
  4. Add mushroom and stir until cook
  5. Add butter, cream, basil, salt and pepper
  6. Stir and cook for 3 more minutes
  7. Mix the sauce with the pasta
